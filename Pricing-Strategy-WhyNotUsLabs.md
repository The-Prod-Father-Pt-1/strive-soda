## PRICING MODELS

### Model A: Traditional (Hourly Only)
**Structure:**
- High initial build cost
- Post-launch support at hourly rate
- No commitment required

**Pros:**
- Maximum revenue per hour
- No ongoing obligations

**Cons:**
- Unpredictable income
- Client may leave after build
- Harder to close deals (high upfront)

**When to Use:** One-off projects, clients who don't want ongoing relationship

---

### Model B: Low Build + Required Retainer â­ RECOMMENDED
**Structure:**
- Reduced initial build cost (40-60% discount)
- Mandatory 6-12 month retainer commitment
- Discounted hourly rate for retainer work

**Example:**
- Standard build: $20,000
- Discounted build: $10,000 (50% off)
- Required: $2,000/month retainer Ã— 12 months = $24,000
- **Total Year 1: $34,000** (vs. $20,000 one-time)
- **Client saves $6,000 upfront, you earn $14,000 more**

**Pros:**
- Easier to close (lower barrier to entry)
- Guaranteed recurring revenue
- Long-term client relationships
- More total revenue over time

**Cons:**
- Lower immediate cash flow
- Requires commitment tracking

**When to Use:** New clients, startups, clients with ongoing needs

---

### Model C: Value-Based Pricing
**Structure:**
- Fixed price based on business impact, not hours
- Monthly retainer for ongoing optimization
- Performance bonuses tied to revenue

**Example:**
- Build: $15,000 flat
- Retainer: $3,000/month + 2% of incremental revenue
- If your work drives $50k extra revenue â†’ +$1,000 bonus

**Pros:**
- Aligned incentives
- Can earn significantly more
- Clients love results-based pricing

**Cons:**
- Harder to predict income
- Requires tracking metrics
- Only works with measurable goals

**When to Use:** E-commerce stores with clear KPIs, growth-stage businesses

## RETAINER STRUCTURES

### Retainer Tier 1: "Maintenance"
**$1,500/month** (5-8 hours included)
- Bug fixes and minor updates
- App integration support
- Email/chat support (48hr response)
- 1 scheduled check-in per month
- Unused hours rollover (max 10 hours)

**Best For:** Small stores, stable sites, minimal changes

**Overage Rate:** $200/hr (vs. $350 standard)

---

### Retainer Tier 2: "Growth" â­ MOST POPULAR
**$3,000/month** (15-20 hours included)
- Everything in Maintenance
- New feature development
- Conversion rate optimization
- A/B testing support
- Priority support (24hr response)
- 2 scheduled strategy calls per month
- Unused hours rollover (max 20 hours)

**Best For:** Growing stores, seasonal businesses, active development

**Overage Rate:** $175/hr (vs. $350 standard)

---

### Retainer Tier 3: "Enterprise"
**$6,000/month** (35-40 hours included)
- Everything in Growth
- Dedicated developer allocation
- Proactive monitoring & optimization
- Strategic planning & consulting
- Same-day emergency support
- Weekly strategy calls
- Quarterly business reviews
- Unlimited rollover hours

**Best For:** High-volume stores, rapid growth, complex needs

**Overage Rate:** $150/hr (vs. $350 standard)

---

### Custom/Project-Based Retainer
**Negotiable** (Client-specific)
- Fixed scope of work per month
- Can combine hours + specific deliverables
- Example: "20 hours/month + 1 new landing page"

**Best For:** Unique situations, specific ongoing projects
